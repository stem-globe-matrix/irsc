About IRSC

The International Research Standards Council is an independent initiative focused on scientific validation, ethical review systems, reproducibility standards, and transparent innovation frameworks.

Mission

To establish accessible and transparent research standards for future scientific and technological innovation.

Vision

Building a globally connected ecosystem where credible research can be validated and openly shared.

Focus

Ethical AI systems, open science, STEM innovation, and scientific reproducibility.

Validation Pipeline

A structured review process ensuring ethical compliance, technical review, and scientific integrity.

1

Research Submission

Research papers, technical documentation, datasets, or innovation reports are formally submitted for evaluation.

2

Technical Review

Subject reviewers analyze methodology, structure, references, technical accuracy, and scientific consistency.

3

Ethics & Integrity Screening

Submitted work undergoes integrity checks including plagiarism review, ethical compliance, and disclosure verification.

4

Validation & Verification

Reproducibility standards, data verification protocols, and technical validation frameworks are applied.

5

Certification & Publication

Approved research receives structured certification identifiers and may be archived within IRSC publication systems.
